About

Ms. Karina Lou is an established piano tutor with 8 years of experience based in Singapore. She holds a Bachelor of Music (Hons) from Goldsmith College of Music and is currently pursuing her Masters of Music Education at National Institute of Education Singapore. She is well versed with the ABRSM syllabus and comfortable teaching all grades for examination attainment or for enjoyment. It brings her joy to see students learning and excited about seeing their progress. She has been given opportunities to perform solo and collaborative performances.
